Engage your heart, make the most of your day

EKG pattern recognition

The Pulseband learns EKG patterns and distinguishes between normal elevated heart rate due to exercise and true cardiac events.

Early detection

With any unusual heart patterns, the user is immediately notified with haptic feedback and a red display. When patterns reach a critical point, the nearest hospital is notified.

Unobtrusive blood pressure readings

Tell the Pulseband how often to check your blood pressure. A gentle haptic response will let you know when your latest reading is available.
